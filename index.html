  • Ramazan Dua For (Fasting)

    Dua (Prayer) at the Beginning of the Fast

    وَبِصَوْمِ غَدٍ نَّوَيْتُ مِنْ شَهْرِ رَمَضَانَ

    Translate:I intend to keep the fast for tomorrow in the month of Ramadan

  • Ramazan Dua For (Iftar)

    Dua (Prayer) at the End Of Fast

    اللَّهُمَّ اِنِّى لَكَ صُمْتُ وَبِكَ امنْتُ وَعَليْكَ تَوَكّلتُ وَ عَلى رِزْقِكَ اَفْطَرْتُ

    Translate:O Allah! I fasted for You and I believe in You and I put my trust in You and I break my fast with Your sustenance

  • First 10 Days Dua

    يَا حَيُّ يَا قَيُّومُ بِرَحْمَتِكَ أَسْتَغيثُ

    Translater:Oh Everliving, The Everlasting, I seek Your help through Your mercy.

    Secend 20 Days Dua

    اَسْتَغْفِرُ اللہَ رَبِّی مِنْ کُلِّ زَنْبٍ وَّ اَتُوْبُ اِلَیْہِ

    Translater:I seek forgiveness from Allah for all my sins and turn to Him.

    Last 30 Days Dua

    اَللَّهُمَّ أَجِرْنِي مِنَ النَّارِ

    Translater:O Allah, save me from the fire (Jahannam).
